About Me

My story:


Hello, and welcome to my portfolio!

Let me share my journey with you in a few lines.
I completed high school as an economic, legal, and commerical technician, believing it would be the best foundation before pursuing my passion for IT at university. During those years, I discovered a deep interest in graphic design, particularly photo editing and creating unique designs using tools like Photoshop and Figma.

I began learning independently by watching tutorial videos, which turned out to be an effective way to develop my skills. Today, I have significant experience in photo manipulation, designing web and app prototypes, and creating various visual projects.
As I progressed through university, my passion for programming grew stronger. Day by day, through numerous projects, I found programming increasingly fascinating. These experiences allowed me to gain knowledge in multiple areas of programming, which you can explore further through the link provided here in my portfolio links-section.
Thank you for taking the time to learn about me!
